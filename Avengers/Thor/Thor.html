Thor Odinson

Thor Odinson is a fictional character portrayed by Chris Hemsworth in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Thor's parents are Frigga and Odin. Who are both currently deceased, Frigga was slain by Algrim during the Dark Elves' raid on Asgard. Odin's death is quite unclear in the MCU, however fans speculate Odin passed away from being exiled from Asgard by the hands of Loki. During Odin's exile, Odin's power slowly faded and eventually disintegrated into pure energy and entered Valhalla. Thor's adopted brother Loki, was slain by Thanos, however during the Avengers Time Heist in the movie Avenger Endgame, Loki managed to escape during a period of time where he was captured. This leads the fans to think Loki's future has also changed, meaning Loki could have possibly survived. Thor then found out he had a older sister, who was hell bent on ruling Asgard and taking over the 9 relms. During his battle with Hela, Thor's beloved hammer mjolnir was destroyed, which caused Thor an identity crisis. Thor soon realized he was the god of thunder and defeated Hela, with the help of Surtur. Thor's whole world fell apart, his home Asgard exploded into peices, and his entire family dead or presumed dead. Thor is now traveling the galaxy with The Guardian's of the Galaxy.
